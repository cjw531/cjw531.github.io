This is an advertisement video for the 368: Android mobile application. This scheduling Android mobile application is a combination of Google Calendar and Doodle. People usually use Google Calendar to manage their personal schedule, and use Doodle to find all available times of several others. With this single application, it allows you to manage not only a person's schedule, but also schedule group meetings. That's where 368 comes in. 368 is here for users to plan their 365 days. And 368 will be right behind users, to get them ahead 3 days. To manage users' 365, and make them feel like they have 368 days.
